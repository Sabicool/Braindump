Take notes with the book closed

Posted on [2025-10-06 Mon 15:39] by Sabiqul Hoque

This is meant to somewhat activate active recall. The idea is that you read the content first, understand it and process it. And then attempt to write your notes afterwards from memory and only after you have exhausted your memory and understanding do you return to the source material to fill in the gaps of your knowledge.
