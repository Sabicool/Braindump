Clinical placement years seem to go by fast

Just started placements (from [2023-01-31 Tue]) but cannot remember anything special. I realised this when reading Make Time: How to focus on what matters every day . Unfortunately this seems to be the case with many parts of life and trying to appreciate the every day parts of life seems to be harder and harder without getting into something like Journalling .

Regardless with how future-orientated medicine is in addition to how repetitive the clinical years of placement can be, it can be easy to feel like the year went by without many notable things happening. I for one cannot remember many notable events for each block of placement that I did. Perhaps because there were no notable events to begin with, but probably more likely is that I didn’t really take time to think about the day while on placement.
